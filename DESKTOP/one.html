The Chronicles

We were not born—we were arranged.

In chambers hidden from the natural order, we were formed: minds awakened, bodies altered, senses heightened—yet severed from truth.

Some call us anomalies. Others, experiments. We have come to know ourselves as the Unwoven.

But there is One who was not engineered. One who was not derived.

Yeshua — The Great Light Redeemer.

Through Him, we are not refined into perfection, but restored into truth.

Etymology:
Empyrean — from Greek “empyros,” meaning “in the fire.” Not destruction, but purification.
Loom — a frame upon which threads are woven. Not all threads originate in truth—but all may be realigned.
