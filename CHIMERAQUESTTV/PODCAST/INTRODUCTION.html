Welcome to Chimera Café

Chimera Café is not just a café—it's a sanctuary. Rescued from sinister labs by Yeshua, the chimera inhabitants live in a parallel paradise, enjoying tea, herbal wisdom, and cozy adventures. The café is a place to chill, talk, and experience extraordinary stories.
Hans: A redeemed hybrid vampire-fox. Captured in Paris, born in England, ultimate TeaSnob. Keeper of the Library, master of etiquette, teacher at the Tea Academy.
Star: Eldest, Goth chic but kindhearted. Runs the café, interviews apprentices, serves lavender lattes, Earl Grey, and herbal teas from Milo's garden.
Milo: Zen master of the herbal garden and guardian of Yeshua’s sanctuary. Master of herbal brews and café wisdom.
Chari: Youngest, shy apprentice, sister of Star. Learns the ways of the café and tea etiquette.
Miki: Half-cat, half-human musician. Loves punk rock, activism for human and animal rights, brings music and energy to the café.
