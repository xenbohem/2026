Royal Meals Scrapbook

Breakfast

Earl Grey tea – a royal morning essential
Served first, milk added after brewing.
Special K cereal – light and healthy
Granary toast with marmalade – breakfast tradition
Scrambled eggs with smoked salmon

Lunch

Grilled Dover sole with seasonal vegetables
Light chicken salad with vinaigrette
Cucumber sandwiches – dainty and proper
Crusts removed for etiquette.

Afternoon Tea

Black tea – afternoon ritual
No tipping of the cup; pinky myths debunked.
Scones with clotted cream and jam
Mini pastries & tarts – sweet treats

Dinner

Fillet of beef with mushroom-whisky sauce
Roasted salmon – healthy and elegant
Chocolate mousse or fresh strawberries
